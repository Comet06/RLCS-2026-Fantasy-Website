Overall Rules

  1. Prizes will be distributed based on the total points at the end of each split and after the championship(3 total, $200 per major, $500 for Championship, Plus additions from contributing players).
  2. Prize pool TBD at the beginning of each season and subject to change.
  3. Any issues with the prize pool can be sent to a league administrator.
  4. Any issues with the website, submission issues, or data tables used for stat tracking or any other documents provided by the league can be submitted to a league administrator for review.
  5. Any issues with 3rd party websites that are not maintained by the league are not to be submitted to the administrator.

Drafting Rules

  1. New Rule: Draft will occur at the beginning of the season, a total of 4 will be drafted. 3 active players and 1 inactive sub
  2. Redraft may occur due to additional members joining the league after Each major and before worlds.
  3. Player trades, swaps and all other changes must be made before both of the regions in question have already played, or have not played yet.
  4. Team and positions will need to be submitted before the regionals have started in order for the changes to take effect, if no submission is made, previous submissions will be in effect.
  5. Championship bracket will need to be submitted before the first kickoff of the first matches.
  6. If there are any issues with the bracket and picks, the admin will make a decision on when brackets can be submitted.

Matchup Rules

  1. New Rule: Each regional you will go up against a different random member in the group.
  2. New Rule: Each matchup will have a cash prize of $10 and 500 points to the winner.
  3. New Rule: Ties are settled by average placement finish of each regional.
  4. New Rule: Prize pools are split between Split 1, Split 2 and Championship and are not Cumulative.
  5. New Rule: In the event of an odd number of Members, a placement team will be created to play against the odd player each event.
